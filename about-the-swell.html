Our tours and adventure trips are catered to each individual. Before the trip, we will contact you, individually, and get to know you, your background, your previous outdoor experience, fears and questions, expectations, and allergies. Each day trip and extended adventure tour will be different, and the unexpected is likely to occur. We want to be prepared beyond what we can generally see. We combine the expertise and know-how of our guides and health assistants with the information we learn from you, to tailor each bend of our journey to you and those who attend in your group; a group arranged by us so that each individual can have a congruent, EXTRAORDINARY experience.

What to Expect

On any given trip you can expect to hike in and around your area to the river site, where you will be given preparatory training on white water rafting, and enter the rafts with one guide on each raft, paddle and cruise through thrilling waters, eat meal(s) outdoors, and enjoy group nature activities. You will have 4-10 guides and 1-4 health assistants with your group at all times.
